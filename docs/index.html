About Me

I am a front-end web developer that ventures into the world of full-stack from time to time. My drive is to create things people can appreciate, and to create them well.

Shortly after graduation, I created my first website. As basic and unresponsive as it was, I was excited by the potential of what coding could do. Since then, I’ve spent over 5 years growing my skills and look forward to my continued growth.

I love to make things and I especially enjoy learning how to make them. When not expanding my knowledge of coding languages, I’ll spend hours researching other miscellaneous skills—like how to render 3D sprinkles and how to turn wool blue. I’m a nerd and a geek, and proud to be both.
